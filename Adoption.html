Dog Adoption!

Adopting a pet is a rewarding experience that brings joy to both the animals and their new families. Here at MDCC (Mandaluyong Dog Clinic with Crematorium), we believe that every animal deserves a loving home. Our adoption program is dedicated to matching you with the perfect companion, whether it's a playful puppy, a cuddly kitten, or a loyal adult pet.

By adopting from us, you are giving a second chance to a wonderful animal in need. Our team ensures that all our adoptable pets are healthy, vaccinated, and ready to join your family. We provide ongoing support and resources to help you and your new pet adjust and thrive together.

Explore our adoption listings to find your new best friend today!
